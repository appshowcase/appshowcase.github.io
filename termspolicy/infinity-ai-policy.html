The Infinity AI app does not require any personal or sensitive information from users. However, the app may use certain anonymous identifiers for the proper functioning of the application, including unique identifiers used to monitor app usage and ensure security.

The text and images that you enter when using the app is processed by OpenAI's DALLE-2, which may use the entered text for training purposes, service improvement, or other related purposes.

Never input or use personal information while using the Infinity AI app.

In-App Purchases:

The Infinity AI app offers in-app purchases for additional features or content. The app does not store or process any payment information directly.

Use of Unique Identifiers:

The Infinity AI app may use unique identifiers, such as device identifiers, cookies, or other similar technologies, to monitor app usage, analyze trends, and ensure the security and integrity of the app. These unique identifiers help in providing a better user experience and maintaining the overall functionality of the app.

User-Provided Images:

The Infinity AI app allows users to upload and process their own images. However, users should not use images that contain personally identifiable information. The app processes these images using OpenAI's technology to provide various functionalities or services within the app.

This app is supported by advertisements. Users can choose whether they want to see personalized or non-personalized ads. If users agree to receive personalized ads, their personal data may be processed through their Advertising ID, and information from their device (such as cookies, unique identifiers, and other device data) may be stored, accessed, or shared with third-party providers, including AdMob. Some providers may process personal data based on a legitimate interest.
